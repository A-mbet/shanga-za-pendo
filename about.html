About Shanga By Pendo

Welcome to Shanga By Pendo! We are a passionate team dedicated to creating unique, handmade hair accessories that celebrate culture and craftsmanship.
Shanga Za Pendo is a celebration of beauty, culture, and craftsmanship. Founded by a passionate artisan from Kenya, our brand brings the vibrant spirit of the Maasai people to life through handmade hair bands and hair pins.
Each accessory is carefully crafted using traditional beadwork techniques passed down through generations. We believe that every strand of hair deserves a touch of love—and every piece we create carries that love.
Whether you're dressing up for a special occasion or adding flair to your everyday look, our accessories are designed to make you feel bold, beautiful, and connected to heritage.
Thank you for being a part of our journey. We invite you to explore our collection and find the perfect accessory that speaks to your unique style and story.

Meet the Maker

Our founder, Pendo, is a skilled artisan with a deep love for traditional beadwork. With years of experience and a passion for creativity, Pendo brings each piece to life with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to quality. Her dedication to preserving cultural heritage through her craft is what inspires every design at Shanga By Pendo.
